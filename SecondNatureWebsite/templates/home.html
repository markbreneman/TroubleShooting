Smart Phones for Science

How It Works

The Second Nature Project transforms donated smartphones into wildlife cameras.

Deploy

Consumers can signup and donate their phones to be deployed into the wild under the supervision of wildlife researchers. When phone takes a picture it notifies the user letting them see how their donation is working.
