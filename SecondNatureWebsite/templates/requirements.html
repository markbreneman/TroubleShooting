Requirements

For smartphones to be a part of the second nature project there are a few minimum requirements that they must meet.

Condition:

Donated Smartphones are expected to be gently used, but not broken. For it to be a useful tool for wildlife conservationists and researchers the phone must still power on, hold a charge, and the screen and a camera of the phone must be in good functioning condition.

Operating System:

Currently the we only support Android phones running 2.3.2 or above. If your unsure of what operating system you have on your phone, you can find out by going to;

Settings->About this phone->System information.

Data and Privacy:

Before sending your phones to The Second Nature we ask that you erase all the data from the phone, to keep your information private. This can be done on your phone by going to;

Settings->Factory Erase
